FAQ

How much is a live-chat system?

Meiqia Pro ~1888/seat/yr with a free tier; Zhichi 8000+; billed by seat/conversation/concurrency depending on fit.

Can Meiqia do overseas support?

Strong on China channels + AI; native WhatsApp/Messenger support is weak (often via 3rd party), best for 'overseas + China'.

High-compliance industry?

Finance/health need on-prem (data stays in-house) + grade-3 security + crypto + audit; SaaS isn't enough.

Youdao or DeepL?

European pro docs DeepL; ZH-EN daily/lookup/keep-layout Youdao reads better (~10% higher naturalness).

How to keep doc layout when translating?

Youdao exports editable Word (layout kept) with bilingual view, good for papers/contracts.
